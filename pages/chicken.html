CHICKEN

How to cook chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, wings, or breasts)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Optional spices: paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper

Instructions

  • Prepare the chicken
    Pat the chicken pieces dry.
    Season them lightly with salt and pepper.
  • Marinate (optional but very helpful)
    Place chicken in a bowl and cover with buttermilk.
    Add a bit of salt, pepper, and optional hot sauce.
    Let it rest for at least 1 hour or up to overnight in the fridge.
  • Prepare the coating
    In a large bowl, mix flour, optional cornstarch, salt, pepper, and any spices you like.
    This is your crispy coating.
  • Coat the chicken
    Remove each piece from the marinade and let excess drip off.
    Dredge it in the flour mixture, pressing the flour onto the chicken to form a thick coating.
    Place coated pieces on a tray and let them rest for 10‚Äì15 minutes (this helps the crust stick).
  • Heat the oil

  • Drain & serve
    Remove chicken and place on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
    Let rest for a few minutes before serving so it stays crisp.

Tips

  • Use bone-in, skin-on chicken for the juiciest results.
  • Resting the coated chicken before frying helps prevent the crust from falling off.
  • Cornstarch in the flour mix makes the crust extra crunchy.
